🎓 How AI is Transforming Education

By Ankit Raj | June 2025

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping how we teach and learn by making education more personalized and accessible.

📚 Personalized Learning

AI tools can adapt content based on student performance and pace, creating customized learning paths.

📊 Automated Grading

Teachers save time with AI-powered grading systems, especially for quizzes and multiple-choice questions.

🌍 Global Access

AI tutors, chatbots, and learning assistants can reach students in rural and remote areas, bridging educational gaps.

"AI isn't replacing teachers. It's helping them teach better." – Ankit Raj
